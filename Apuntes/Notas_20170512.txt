Conceptos:
 - Que es Cloud: paradigma que permite ofrecer servicios a través de internet.

Distintas capas
 - PaaS: Plataform as a Service. (iOS, Android, Web)
 - Saas: Software as a Service. (Aplicaciones: Imagen Windows Server)
 - IaaS: Infraestructura as a Service. (Amazon: CPDs)
 - BaaS MBaaS: Movile Backend as a Service. (PARSE)
